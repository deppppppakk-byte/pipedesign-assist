Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

1. Overview

Scroll Blocker is designed to help users reduce endless scrolling and improve focus. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

2. Accessibility Service Usage

Scroll Blocker may use Android Accessibility Service only to detect selected distracting screens or app usage patterns and apply blocking or reminder rules chosen by the user.

The Accessibility Service is not used to read private messages, passwords, payment details, contacts, photos, media files, or personal content.

3. Data Collection

By default, Scroll Blocker is designed to work locally on the device. The app should not collect or sell personal data. If you later add analytics, login, cloud backup, crash reporting, or ads, this policy must be updated before publishing.

4. Local App Data

The app may store selected settings locally on the device, such as selected apps, time limits, blocking preferences, and focus progress.

5. Third-Party Services

This website template does not include third-party tracking by default. If third-party services are added later, they must be listed here.

6. User Control

Users can disable the Accessibility Service from Android Settings at any time. Users can also uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended to collect personal information from children. If the app is later targeted to children, additional legal and safety requirements may apply.
